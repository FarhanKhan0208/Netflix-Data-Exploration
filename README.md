#🎬 Netflix Data Exploration & Visualization📌 
*Project Overview
This project performs an in-depth analysis of the Netflix movies and TV shows dataset to understand the platform's content strategy. By utilizing Python and data visualization libraries, this notebook explores the growth of content, regional production differences, and rating trends.
*❓ Questions Addressed
The notebook provides analysis and visualizations to answer questions such as:
What is the trend of movie vs. TV show releases over the years?
Which countries produce the most content on Netflix?
What are the top genres available on the platform?
How has the distribution of content ratings changed over time?
